Lesson 2: Grammar Rules

Understand the grammar rules of the language, including verb conjugation and sentence structure.

  • Grammar Rule 1: Use active voice.
  • Grammar Rule 2: Link ideas with a conjunction.
  • Grammar Rule 3: Use a comma to connect two ideas
  • Grammar Rule 4: Use a serial comma in a list.
  • Grammar Rule 5: When writing, use a semicolon to join two ideas
  • Grammar Rule 6: Use the Simple Present Tense for habitual actions.
  • Grammar Rule 7: Use the Present Continuous Tense for current actions
  • Grammar Rule 8: When talking about past actions, don’t forget that there are regular and irregular verbs.
  • Grammar Rule 9: Use the Present Perfect Tense with words or expressions of unfinished time:
  • Grammar Rule 10: Use the Present Perfect Continuous Tense when the action has not finished as well:
